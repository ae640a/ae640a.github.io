This course is graded on a straight scale, it will not be curved. There are a total of 100 possible points in this course. You are expected to earn 40 points from Homework Assignments, 40 points from Project, 5 points from Attendance and 15 points from Mid-Semester Examination. However each section has modest opportunity for extra credit that can cover missing points elsewhere.

Range notation [90,93) means 90 is included and 93 is not

{% assign anchor_text="Homework (40% / 40 points)" %} {% assign anchor_name="syllabus-homework" %} {% assign anchor_html_entity="h3" %} {% include anchor.html %}

There are 5 homework assignments in the course. Each homework assignment is worth 8 points. There might be bonus questions in each assignment carrying extra points. Any points over 40 points are worth half their value.

Late assignments will not be accepted.


{% assign anchor_text="Attendance and Participation (5% / 5 points)" %} {% assign anchor_name="syllabus-attendance" %} {% assign anchor_html_entity="h3" %} {% include anchor.html %}

Attendance is not compulsory for this course. Bring your laptop to every lecture. Lectures might include some quick quizzes used both to verify comprehension and attendance. Generally, correct answers will be worth full credit and incorrect answers half credit (you were there, but didn’t get it right). Generally, we will ask at least three questions during class. Not all questions may count for attendance credit. We reserve the right to experiment with this mechanism a bit throughout the semester and vary when and how we ask questions or validate attendance.
